TAZLARM v2.2.4 implemented scope

This build preserves every feature and reliability fix from v2.2.3 and updates the live alarm layout:

- Moves the TAZLARM sneezing-cat header to the top safe area of the alarm screen.
- Reduces the header height from the previous oversized layout to a compact 64dp brand row.
- Reduces top-level vertical padding so the live content starts immediately below the status bar.
- Makes the question, barcode and photo challenge card narrower and more compact.
- Reduces internal card padding, spacing, icon sizes and button heights while keeping touch targets usable.
- Preserves more visible sunrise animation around and below the challenge card.
- Removes the live alarm progress bar completely.
- Keeps the two-minute minimum sunrise and no sunrise countdown.
- Updates the app version to 2.2.4 / versionCode 224.
