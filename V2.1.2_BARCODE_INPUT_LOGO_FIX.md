# v2.1.2 barcode, input and logo fix

## Why the barcode could appear different

The earlier alarm screen compared only `scanned.rawValue == saved.rawValue`. The same physical retail barcode can sometimes be surfaced as UPC-A (12 digits) on one scan and EAN-13 with a leading zero on another. Invisible whitespace can also differ. A missing registration was also reported as a generic mismatch.

## Fix

- Registration stores format, raw bytes, raw text and display text.
- Matching tries raw bytes, normalised text and canonical 14-digit GTIN representation.
- Old plain-string registrations still work.
- Missing registration receives a specific message.
- The barcode card includes a visible 50-question recovery route.
- Presets and new alarms retain configured barcode/photo data.

## Input and logo

- Alarm answer text, cursor, label and border are black on white.
- The exact supplied sneezing-cat TAZALARM artwork is embedded as transparent assets and used across the app and launcher.
