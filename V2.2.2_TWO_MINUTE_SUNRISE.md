# TAZLARM v2.2.2 two-minute sunrise

- Sunrise animation now lasts at least 120 seconds for every alarm.
- Existing saved alarms below 120 seconds are automatically migrated to 120 seconds when the app opens.
- New alarms default to a 120-second sunrise.
- Alarm editing enforces a 120-second minimum and still allows longer durations up to 600 seconds.
- The live alarm screen no longer displays a sunrise countdown or remaining-time timer.
- The routine progress bar remains because it tracks challenge completion, not sunrise time.
