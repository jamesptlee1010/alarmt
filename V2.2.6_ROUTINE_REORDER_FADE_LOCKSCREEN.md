# TAZLARM v2.2.6 routine reorder, transition and lock-screen build

This build adds:

- Press-and-hold vertical drag reordering for routine cards.
- A visible drag handle, lift/scale treatment and haptic feedback while reordering.
- Existing Up and Down buttons remain available on both the Home routine list and Routine editor.
- The selected light-mode Home layout with readiness, next alarm, today’s routine and last-result cards.
- After the final alarm stage, TAZLARM switches to light mode and fades from black into Home over two seconds.
- Step-specific quiet windows before ringing and vibration resume:
  - Questions: 7 seconds whenever a new question appears.
  - Barcode: 20 seconds when the barcode stage appears.
  - Photo verification: 30 seconds when the photo stage appears.
- Sound and vibration both pause, then resume at the current alarm volume level.
- Immersive full-screen alarm operation over the Android lock screen.
- Android 14+ full-screen-intent permission status and direct settings action.

Secure Android lock screens are not silently unlocked. The alarm activity can run above the lock screen while active; after completion, a secure device may require normal authentication before the regular Home activity remains accessible.
